# 19. The Board Pitch

### Executive Board Pitch: FY26 Security Capital Request

**Where We Are Today**

Over the past four weeks, our comprehensive security assessment revealed that MedDefense operates with a flat network, unpatched perimeters, and zero real-time breach detection. We have suffered three cyber incidents in six months—including a four-day ransomware outage. **The verdict is clear: MedDefense is operating on borrowed time.**

**What Is at Stake**

If we do nothing, our statistical exposure is **$4.94 million in expected annual losses**. A single automated ransomware attack will encrypt our Electronic Health Record (EHR) database, halt patient care for eleven days, and cost **$1.8 million** in direct recovery fees, HIPAA fines, and diverted revenue.

**What We Recommend**

We request approval for a targeted **$120,000 security investment** to fund a defense-in-depth model built on six proven controls:

1. **Multi-Factor Authentication ($10,000):** Locks our virtual front doors against stolen passwords.
2. **Network Segmentation ($25,000):** Builds internal firewalls so an infection in a staff clinic cannot reach critical patient databases or medical devices.
3. **Enterprise SIEM & Offsite Backups ($51,000):** Delivers centralized threat monitoring and immutable offsite cloud backups to guarantee recovery without paying ransom.

These core investments stop automated attacks in their tracks and close our highest-priority vulnerabilities within 90 days.

**The Return**

This $120,000 spend slashes our annual loss exposure by **$4.66 million**—delivering a **94.3% risk reduction**. For every **$1 invested today, MedDefense avoids $38.83 in expected risk losses**, protecting both our clinical operations and our financial solvency.
